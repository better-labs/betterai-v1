## Before You Contact Us

To help us assist you more quickly, please consider:

1. **Check the Documentation**: Browse our guides and FAQs - your question might already be answered!

2. **Be Specific**: Include details about:
   - What you were trying to do
   - What happened instead
   - Any error messages you received
   - Your browser and device information (if relevant)

3. **Screenshots**: If you're experiencing a visual issue, screenshots can be very helpful.
